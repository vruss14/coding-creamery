Here's why we cream the competition:

Local Ingredients

Our community has given us so much, and we believe in giving back. All of our ingredients are sourced locally. Interested in partnering with us?

Eco-Friendly

We deeply care about the environment. We sell reusable bowls and spoons that can be used at any one of our locations, and we offer a 10% discount to anyone who uses or brings in reusable bowls and spoons. We also offer disposable, biodegradable bowls and spoons.

Unique Flavors

Our flavors are inspired by the computer science community. Have an idea for a new flavor? We'd love to hear it!

Dairy Free Options

Can't have dairy? No problem at all. We offer a non-dairy version of each of the above flavors. If you have additional dietary restrictions, let us know. We are happy to accommodate and have made different versions of our mix-in ingredients and toppings so that anyone can enjoy our delicious ice cream flavors. Whether you are looking for a dairy-free/vegan, sugar-free, gluten-free, or other option, we've got you covered.
